Event Title

The first meeting of Samaj was held at the premises of Manji Arjan and Company where Mr. Karsanbhai Devraj Vekaria of gaam Baladia was appointed as the First Chairman. The main objective of this meeting was to build a community (Samaj) building in Mombasa, Kenya and raise funds for the same.
